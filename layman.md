Cheatsense is a command-line tool that reads game session log files and automatically detects players who are likely cheating. It works by looking for telltale signs such as reaction times faster than any human can achieve, perfectly robotic click timing, instant aim-lock snaps onto targets, and action speeds no person could sustain. It produces a plain report showing each player's cheat-likelihood score and exactly which suspicious patterns triggered a flag, making it straightforward for game developers or server admins to review and act on the evidence.
